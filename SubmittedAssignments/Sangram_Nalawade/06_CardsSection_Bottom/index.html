NUTRIENTS

What are Nutrients?

A nutrient is a substance used by an organism to survive, grow, and reproduce. The requirement for dietary nutrient intake applies to animals, plants, fungi, and protists. .

protein

Proteins are large biomolecules and macromolecules that comprise one or more long chains of amino acid residues..

carb

Carbs provide your body with energy. One of the primary functions of carbohydrates is to provide energy.

fat

Fats are the source of energy in food, belong to a group of elements called lipids,saturated and unsaturated fats
